Coffee Slide is a fast-paced, physics-based arcade game inspired from suika games. 
Players must slide coffee cups across the counter to reach customers without spilling or overshooting.

## How to Play
1. **Touch/Click** the coffee cup.
2. **Swipe** to give it momentum.
3. **Release** at the right time to stop it at the designated zone.
